Forever Yours

February 14, 2026
My Dearest Puspa,

As I write these words, my heart overflows with a love so deep that no language could ever capture it fully. You are not just the woman I married — you are the reason my life has meaning.

From the moment I first saw you, I knew my world would never be the same. Your eyes held a promise of forever, and your smile painted colors into a life I didn't know was grey.

Do you remember our wedding day? April 24, 2021 — the day the universe finally made sense. When I placed the sindoor on your maang, I wasn't just following tradition. I was making a vow with every fiber of my being: that I would love you, protect you, and stand by you through every sunrise and every storm.

Every journey we have taken together — every mountain we have climbed, every waterfall we have stood before, every sunrise we have chased — has only deepened my gratitude. Because the most beautiful view in the world will always be the one where you are standing beside me.

You are my strength when I am weak. You are my peace when the world is chaos. You are my home, no matter where we are. I may look like I am standing tall on those mountain tops, but the truth is, without you, I would crumble.

This Valentine's Day, I don't just want to say "I love you." I want you to feel it in every heartbeat, in every word on this page, in every photo that tells our story. You are the love of my life, my ardhangini, my forever.

I love you more than words could ever say, more than the mountains are tall, more than the rivers are deep. And I will spend every remaining day of my life proving it to you.

Forever and always yours,
Saroj
